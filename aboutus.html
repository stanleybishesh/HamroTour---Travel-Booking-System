ABOUT US

Hamro Tour was created as a easy online guide website for your future hoildays – a place where you can find honest advice about traveling to our country, as well as ideas and resources to help you plan your perfect holiday. Our mission is to inspire and inform, so that you can make the most of your time with us. We’re supported by a network of professionals with a wealth of knowledge and experience, including some of the leading guides in Nepalese travel team. Affordability is always in our thoughts. We believe that tourism should be a force for good, both in terms of the natural environment and the people and communities who live in our country. All of the tours and services you’ll find links to on this site operate with the highest standards of quality and sustainability, as well as directly benefiting Nepali communities and businesses. The Hamro Tour team are locally based and on hand to help you find the perfect options for your holiday.
